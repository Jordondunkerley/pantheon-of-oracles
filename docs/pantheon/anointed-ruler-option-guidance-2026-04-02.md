# Anointed Ruler Option Guidance

## New understanding captured
The anointed ruler path should present up to four selectable logic options, each with concise guidance:
- Traditional Ruler
- Modern Ruler
- Solar Ruler
- Dominant Ruler

## Guidance intent
- Solar is the fallback for players without a birth time
- Dominant gives those players a second non-basic option
- Traditional and Modern remain available and self-explanatory
- Players with full birth data are still allowed to choose any option; no lockout

## Product effect
This keeps ruler selection clearer, more player-friendly, and more aligned with real Pantheon usage logic.
