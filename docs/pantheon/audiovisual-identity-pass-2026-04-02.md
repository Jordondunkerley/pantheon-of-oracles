# Audiovisual Identity Pass

## Why this pass mattered
The chamber needed stronger visual identity even before sound, motion, or full cinematic treatment. Without it, the app still risked looking like a dark UI rather than a world.

## What changed
- richer layered background treatment
- stronger glow/contrast hierarchy
- more ceremonial gold accent usage
- stronger selected-state and card-surface presence
- softer, more atmospheric visual depth in chamber scenes

## Product effect
This pushes the app further away from plain dark-dashboard presentation and closer to a recognisable chamber aesthetic.
