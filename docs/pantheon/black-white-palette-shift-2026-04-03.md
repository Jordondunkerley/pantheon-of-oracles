# Black / White Palette Shift

## Why this pass mattered
The current blue-heavy dark palette was working against the desired premium look. A black background with white and light-grey text is cleaner, more serious, and better aligned with the requested visual direction.

## What changed
- shifted the core palette toward black backgrounds
- changed primary text to white
- changed subtext to light grey
- removed blue-heavy input/button surface treatment in favor of neutral dark surfaces

## Product effect
This immediately moves the chamber toward a cleaner, more premium, more serious visual identity with less obvious fantasy-dashboard coloration.
