# Density and Milestone Pass

## Why this pass mattered
Even with scene structure and reveal language, the product still risked feeling too dense and too informational. A game-like chamber needs cleaner scenes and visible milestone states.

## What changed
- reduced scene density further by trimming secondary surfaces out of some scenes
- added milestone cards to progression nav
- surfaced complete / active / locked milestone status
- pushed more emphasis onto progression and chamber momentum over admin density

## Product effect
This makes the build feel more guided and less like a compressed multi-panel app squeezed into scene wrappers.
