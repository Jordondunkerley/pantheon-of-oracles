# Palette Propagation Pass

## Why this pass mattered
Changing the root palette was not enough by itself. The scene surfaces, hero, and cards also needed to reflect the black/white premium direction or the old blue-heavy feel would persist.

## What changed
- shifted hero, scene backgrounds, and card surfaces toward neutral black/dark-grey treatment
- reduced the remaining blue-heavy visual texture across the main front-end surfaces

## Product effect
This makes the black/white premium direction read consistently across the whole app instead of only in isolated variables.
