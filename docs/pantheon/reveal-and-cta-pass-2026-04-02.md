# Reveal and CTA Pass

## Why this pass mattered
Progression gating alone still risks feeling mechanical. To feel more like a game, the chamber also needs stronger language and reveal behavior.

## What changed
- scene buttons now use ritualized/game-like CTA wording
- title screen and scene cards now frame the next step as a rite rather than a task
- reveal-card animation layer added
- scene transitions now carry more visible distinction between hidden and active states

## Product effect
This strengthens the sense that the player is advancing through a chamber journey rather than clicking between admin tabs.

## Next likely priorities
1. reduce information density inside scenes further
2. add stronger milestone/reward/unlock feedback
3. validate locally and push updated checkpoint
